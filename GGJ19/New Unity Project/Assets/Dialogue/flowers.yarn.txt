"They're called Forget-Me-Nots," they said, eyes twinkling in the sunlight. There was a great blue field that always seemed to sway like ocean waves. It went on forever. They were her favorite bloom, always donning her head like a crown. Such a delicate bloom for such a powerful woman. You remember watching her way of handling a knife, the way she knew how to protect herself from dangers. "An Adventurer knows when to fight and when to flee," she would say. "But knowing when to rest is important too."
